# Maintainers

## Vincent Paul Del Greco — Founder & Lead Maintainer

I am currently an active duty U.S. Navy officer with graduate degrees in Architectural Engineering Technology, Construction Management, and Architecture. My professional life has been spent in high-stakes institutional environments — DoD, contracting, major construction projects, and private sector firms in the construction industry — where explaining the reasoning behind decisions is not optional. When the stakes are high, reviews and reasoning demand greater scrutiny.

That discipline is what drove me to build COR Proof. When I began integrating AI heavily into my workflows in 2025, I kept running into the same gap: fluent outputs with no inspectable reasoning behind them. COR Proof is my answer to that gap.

I founded OmnySync Group LLC and the Stake The Truth initiative to bring open standards for AI reasoning transparency into the world. With my full-time job and busy family — this project runs on every spare hour I have, and I'm committed to this for the long haul - the future demands it.

I welcome contributors and maintainers who share the conviction that reasoning should be inspectable.
